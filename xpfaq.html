Is it free?

Yes. You pay for the Windows license (product key), not shown in the video. You enter your product key during the Windows installation. Activation just verifies that your Windows license is genuine. During activation, you only pay for the call (if you don't call a toll-free number) or the Internet connection.
Look at it this way: Windows activation = Windows license verification
