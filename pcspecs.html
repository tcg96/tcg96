(*) The reason some of my iPhone screen shots look like they're from an iPhone 6 or 6S (not 7 because I don't have iOS 10) is because of a jailbreak tweak called "Upscale" that enables the iPhone 6/7 (not Plus) screen resolution on any device running iOS 8 or newer. You'd think it makes everything look tiny and blurry on the iPhone 5 screen, but it's actually not bad at all and 100% usable. It even works on the iPhone 4S, but it looks squished there because there's a difference in aspect ratio.
